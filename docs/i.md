# 1. Agree / Disagree Essay Template

### Question Type

> To what extent do you agree or disagree?

---

## Introduction

```text id="ad1"
In recent years, [topic] has become a widely debated issue. 
Some people believe that [statement], while others hold a different opinion. 
Personally, I completely agree/disagree with this view for several reasons.
```

---

## Body Paragraph 1

```text id="ad2"
The primary reason is that [main idea]. 
This is because [explanation]. 
For example, [example]. 
As a result, [result].
```

---

## Body Paragraph 2

```text id="ad3"
Another important point is that [main idea]. 
In other words, [explanation]. 
For instance, [example]. 
Therefore, [result].
```

---

## Conclusion

```text id="ad4"
In conclusion, I strongly agree/disagree that [statement] because [reason 1] and [reason 2].
```

# 2. Advantages & Disadvantages Essay Template

### Question Type

> What are the advantages and disadvantages?

---

## Introduction

```text id="av1"
Nowadays, [topic] is becoming increasingly common around the world. 
Although this trend has several benefits, it also has some drawbacks.
```

---

## Advantages Paragraph

```text id="av2"
One major advantage is that [advantage]. 
This is because [explanation]. 
For example, [example]. 
Consequently, [positive result].
```

---

## Disadvantages Paragraph

```text id="av3"
However, there are also some disadvantages associated with [topic]. 
One significant drawback is that [disadvantage]. 
For instance, [example]. 
As a result, [negative result].
```

---

## Conclusion

```text id="av4"
In conclusion, despite some disadvantages, I believe that the advantages/disadvantages of [topic] are more significant.
```

# 3. Discussion Essay Template

### Question Type

> Discuss both views and give your opinion.

---

## Introduction

```text id="ds1"
People have different views regarding [topic]. 
Some believe that [view 1], while others argue that [view 2]. 
This essay will discuss both perspectives before presenting my own opinion.
```

---

## Body Paragraph 1 (View 1)

```text id="ds2"
On the one hand, many people think that [view 1]. 
This is mainly because [explanation]. 
For example, [example]. 
Therefore, [result].
```

---

## Body Paragraph 2 (View 2)

```text id="ds3"
On the other hand, others believe that [view 2]. 
In other words, [explanation]. 
For instance, [example]. 
As a result, [result].
```

---

## Opinion + Conclusion

```text id="ds4"
In conclusion, although both views have merit, I believe that [your opinion] because [reason].
```

# 4. Problem & Solution Essay Template

### Question Type

> What problems does this cause?
> What are the solutions?

---

## Introduction

```text id="ps1"
In recent years, [problem/topic] has become a serious concern in many countries. 
This issue can lead to several problems, but some effective solutions can be taken to address it.
```

---

## Problems Paragraph

```text id="ps2"
One major problem caused by [topic] is that [problem]. 
This happens because [reason]. 
For example, [example]. 
As a consequence, [negative result].
```

---

## Solutions Paragraph

```text id="ps3"
To solve this issue, governments and individuals should [solution]. 
In addition, [another solution]. 
For instance, [example]. 
Therefore, these measures can significantly reduce the problem.
```

---

## Conclusion

```text id="ps4"
In conclusion, although [topic] creates several challenges, appropriate actions can help overcome these problems effectively.
```

# 5. Double Question Essay Template

### Question Type

> Why is this happening?
> Is this a positive or negative development?

---

## Introduction

```text id="dq1"
Nowadays, [topic] is becoming increasingly common in many parts of the world. 
This essay will discuss the reasons behind this trend and explain why I believe it is a positive/negative development.
```

---

## Reasons Paragraph

```text id="dq2"
One main reason for this trend is that [reason]. 
This is because [explanation]. 
For example, [example]. 
As a result, [result].
```

---

## Positive/Negative Paragraph

```text id="dq3"
I believe this is a positive/negative development because [main idea]. 
In other words, [explanation]. 
For instance, [example]. 
Therefore, [result].
```

---

## Conclusion

```text id="dq4"
In conclusion, this trend is mainly caused by [reason], and I believe it has a positive/negative impact on society.
```
